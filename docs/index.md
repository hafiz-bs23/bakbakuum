## About this site

Bakbakuum is a **technical blog** built with MkDocs Material. Here you will find writing on **Python and backend engineering** (APIs, typing, structure), **Docker and day-to-day tooling**, **software craft** (documentation, quality, how we work), and **AI engineering** (prompt and context design, LLM workflows).

Posts are written for engineers who care about substance over hype—readable examples, trade-offs, and patterns that survive real projects.
